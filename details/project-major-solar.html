Smart AI System for Solar Power Extraction

Project Description

Under different environmental conditions, the current maximum power point tracking techniques are unable to extract the maximum power from solar panels. The conventional techniques results in low accuracy during partial shading conditions as it fails to differentiate between the global maximum power point and local maximum power point. On the other hand, the response time of advanced AI based algorithms is relatively slow during non-partial shading conditions. These results in lesser efficiency for solar PV system. To bridge the gap, this project combines the conventional Incremental Conductance and advanced AI based Particle Swarm Optimization maximum power point tracking techniques to extract the maximum power during different environmental conditions. The proposed technique first detects if partial shading has occurred and executes the proper technique according to the environmental condition. Thus, the solar PV system is able to operate with maximum power effectively at different environmental conditions. The design was verified using MATLAB Simulink and the results prove that the proposed technique works accurately and precisely.

Project information

  • Improved the efficiency of solar PV systems by up to 95% against partial shading conditions by integrating conventional maximum power point tracking with AI-based optimisation.
  • Extensively tested and validated using Matlab and Simulink for real-world conditions and the results proved the accuracy of the system.
